About "Sri Krishna Gopal Sarvkaramchari Vitt Sarthi Gaumata Sewa Charitable Trust, Panipat (Regd. No. 3572)

In this regard, it is brought to your kind knowledge that "Sri Krishna Gopal Sarvkaramchari Vitt Sarthi Gau Sewa Charitable Trust, Panipat (Regd. No. 3572)" has been got registered on 13.07.2022 with the cooperation of the employees to help or to donate the "Gaushala" and also working for prevention of the cruelty against the stray animals or birds. For this, it has been decided to collect one rupee per day means Rs. 30 per month from all the officials /officers of Haryana Roadways, Panipat on purely voluntarily basis. The above collection is being used to help the pathetic condition of the "Gau Mata" or develop the Gaushala and also preservation and welfare of stray animals and birds. And further it is also submitted that many social workers, employees and officers of the other Department are also joining and cooperating the above society. As such, approximately 1000 members have joined the "Sri Krishna Gopal Sarvkaramchari Vitt Sarthi Gau Sewa Charitable Trust, Panipat" till now. And further it is also submitted that total donation of Rs. 280000/- has been received in this society till now and approximately Rs.200000/- donated in the various Gaushalas for feed/Chara and medicines purpose.
